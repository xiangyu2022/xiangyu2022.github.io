Research Focus

My research develops statistically reliable and computationally practical methods for scientific data analysis. Current themes include distribution-free smooth tests, data-driven goodness-of-fit procedures, model validation for angular power spectra in stochastic gravitational-wave background analyses, high-background X-ray spectroscopy, and generative AI applications in insurance.
