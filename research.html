Research

Xiangyu Zhang
Ph.D. Candidate, School of Statistics, University of Minnesota

Research Focus

My research develops statistically reliable and computationally practical methods for scientific data analysis. Current themes include distribution-free smooth tests, data-driven goodness-of-fit procedures, stochastic gravitational-wave background analyses, and high-background X-ray spectroscopy.
